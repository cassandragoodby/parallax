When the moon finally rises after a long day it is finally time for the turtles to be hatched.

The baby turtle hatches at night out of their nest. They have been incubated for two months.

The baby turtles stretch to the ocean is the most trecherous night for a baby turtle. The baby turtle is supposed to follow the moonlight to find the ocean. Because of the lights from cities many baby turtles get lost. Thankfully our little buddy makes it tonight!
